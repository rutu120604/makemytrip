Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a flight on Make My Trip?
You can book a flight by visiting our website, entering your travel details, selecting a flight, and making payment online.
2. Can I cancel my booking and get a refund?
Yes, you can cancel your booking through your account. Refund policies vary depending on the airline and fare type.
3. How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support through our helpline, email, or live chat available on our website.
4. Do I need to create an account to book a trip?
No, you can book as a guest, but having an account allows for easier booking management and exclusive discounts.
5. How do I reschedule my flight?
You can reschedule your flight by logging into your account, selecting your booking, and choosing the "Reschedule" option.
