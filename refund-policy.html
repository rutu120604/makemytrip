Refund Policy

1. Refunds are processed as per the service provider's guidelines.

2. Refunds for flights and hotels are credited within 7-10 working days.

3. Service fees and taxes may not be refundable.

4. Partial refunds apply based on the cancellation terms.

5. Refund requests must be made through the user's account or customer service.

6. Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method used.
