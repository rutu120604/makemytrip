Cancellation Policy

1. Flight cancellations are subject to airline-specific policies.

2. Hotel booking cancellations depend on the hotel's terms and conditions.

3. Bus and train ticket cancellations are allowed based on the transport provider's rules.

4. Some bookings may be non-cancellable or partially refundable.

5. Cancellations made within 24 hours of departure may not be eligible for a refund.

6. MakeMyTrip reserves the right to modify cancellation policies at any time.
