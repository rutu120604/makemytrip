Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Make My Trip. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to enhance your experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address, etc.
  • Payment Details: Credit/Debit card details (processed securely).
  • Travel Preferences: Flight, hotel, visa, and package preferences.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device ID, cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and manage bookings for flights, hotels, and visa applications.
  • Process payments securely.
  • Improve website functionality and user experience.
  • Send personalized promotions and offers.
  • Prevent fraudulent activities and ensure security.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your data with:

  • Payment gateways for secure transactions.
  • Partnered airlines, hotels, and visa service providers.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences.
  • Provide personalized content.
  • Analyze website traffic and improve performance.

6. Security Measures

We take strong security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request data corrections.
  • Opt out of marketing emails.
  • Delete your account (subject to legal retention policies).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with the updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@makemytrip.com.
